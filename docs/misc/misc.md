# Miscellaneous Problems

Sometimes we stuble upon problems that lead to pleasant solutions. Mathematicians will describe them as _elegant_. I've avoided that descriptor due to my particular fondness for the jank and trivial.
This section will contain a mixed bag of scraps from unfinished projects and solutions to textbook problems.
